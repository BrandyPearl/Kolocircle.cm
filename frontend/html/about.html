About KoloCircle

Reimagining the Cameroonian Njangi for the smartphone generation.

Our story

From village circles to your phone.

KoloCircle was born in Buea by Anglophone Cameroonians who grew up watching mothers, aunties and traders run Njangi books by candlelight. We knew the system worked and we knew it could work better.

Today, KoloCircle digitises every step of the Njangi: forming the group, verifying members, collecting contributions through MTN MoMo, Orange Money, UBA, Afriland and NFC Bank and paying out the recipient on cycle day, automatically.
