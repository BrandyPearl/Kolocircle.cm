How it works

From sign-up to payout in four clear steps.

01

Sign up & verify

Register with your phone number, upload your CNI (Carte Nationale d'Identité) and complete a quick face scan.

02

Create or join a circle

Spin up a new Njangi or join one using a 6-digit invite code from a trusted organiser.

03

Set the rules

Decide the contribution amount (XAF), the cycle frequency (weekly / monthly), payout order and group size (3–30 members).

04

Pool & payout

On cycle day, KoloCircle auto-debits each member via MoMo / Orange / bank and the recipient receives the full pool instantly.
