About KoloCircle

A century-old Cameroonian tradition, finally digital.

From Bamenda to Buea, Douala to Yaoundé Njangi has funded school fees, market stalls, weddings and homes for generations. KoloCircle preserves the trust and removes the headaches: missed contributions, misplaced ledgers, and untraceable organisers.
